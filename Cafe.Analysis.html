  • TRIM to remove extra spaces in the text fields.
  • REMOVE DUPLICATES to eliminate redundant records.
  • IFERROR to manage calculation errors.
  • Text inconsistencies were corrected using PROPOER and manual review.
This process ensured that the dataset was accurate, consistent, and ready for analysis.

Creating Pivot Tables:
