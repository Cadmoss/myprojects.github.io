My career journey began in administrative and data entry roles, where I developed a strong attention to detail and process management. Over time, I gravitated toward data analytics, mastering tools such as Microsoft Excel, Power BI, SQL, and Tableau.

My experience spans reporting automation, sales forecasting, business intelligence, and performance analytics. I take pride in translating raw data into meaningful insights that stakeholders can act upon.
